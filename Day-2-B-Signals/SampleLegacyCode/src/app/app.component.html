ngOnChanges — What computed() Replaced

Legacy: ngOnChanges(changes: SimpleChanges) fires when @Input() reference changes. Developers used it to manually recalculate derived values.
Modern replacement: computed() — no lifecycle hook needed. Derived values update automatically when their signal dependencies change.
